Etherix Server FAQ

To claim land, you need a Golden Shovel:

  • Right-click one corner of the area you want to claim.
  • Right-click the opposite corner.

The land between these two points will now be claimed.

To allow someone to build or interact on your claim, use:

/trust <playername>

To remove all your claims, use: /abandonallclaims

Use: /daily

When you claim your reward, you will receive a Daily Crate Key. Use it at the Crates area at Survival Spawn.

  • /back – Return to your previous location
  • /enderchest – Open your Ender Chest anywhere
  • /hat – Put any item on your head
  • /near – See players near you
  • /workbench – Open a crafting table anywhere
  • [VIP] – Special rank in chat
  • VIP Crate Key – 1 per 24 hours using /viprewards
